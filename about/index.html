If Classic & Muscle Cars Are Your Passion, Welcome Home

Vanguard Motor Sales is the nation’s premier classic and muscle car dealership. With over 20 years in business, we’ve earned a reputation as the most trusted car dealer in America. Our mission? Parking your dream in your driveway.

Why Vanguard?

Choosing Vanguard Motor Sales means partnering with a dealership that prioritizes quality and builds lasting relationships. Its about enjoying the complete experience, from working with the Vanguard team, to cruising the open road for years to come. We provide peace of mind every step of the way. Firmly rooted in the Motor City, there's no better destination for buying or selling your American collector car.

We Carry Quality Inventory

We take pride in owning our entire inventory - no consignment here. Each vehicle is carefully handpicked, showcasing our commitment to quality and attention to detail. We travel nationwide to find the best vehicles that meet our strict standards. We are highly meticulous about paint quality, undercarriage condition, and the overall state of each vehicle. When you purchase a Vanguard car, you are investing in our standard of exceptional quality.

We Service Our Vehicles

Our team of expert mechanics fully service each vehicle, providing a level of quality that sets us apart from everyone else. Unlike purchasing from an auction or another dealer, where cars often go uninspected and unserviced, we save you from the headaches that cost you time and money. We do not restore cars; instead, we focus on servicing each vehicle in our inventory. Every car undergoes a full inspection and test drive, after which we thoroughly complete the work order. All of our work is performed in-house by our team of 15 world-class mechanics, who specialize in various areas of repair to ensure every vehicle meets our high standards.
